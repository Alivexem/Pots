POTS AND PANS

HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to an AlivexemTech Game of POTS and PANS. This is a game against A.I (artificial Intelligence) where the A.I picks four random numbers in the range 0 - 20. Your aim is to guess the numbers and arrange the numbers accordingly using the hint provided.

The hint provided is the range of A.I selection (smallest to Largest Number) and most importantly POTS AND PANS.

POT means one of the numbers you guessed is correct and placed in the right order, PAN means you guessed a number right but wrongly placed. For example:


A.I SELECTS 1-2-3-4

AND YOUR GUESS IS 1-4-2-8.


The game response will be 1 POT and 2 PANS because 1 is correct and rightly placed which makes it a POT, 4 and 2 are also correct but wrongly placed which results in PAN. ENOUGH TALK... CHECK IT OUT YOURSELF!
